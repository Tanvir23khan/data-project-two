What is ESG?

The ESG is a Risk Score that is composed of the Environment Risk Score, the Social Risk Score and the Governance Risk Score. We adopt the ESG Risk Rating from Sustainanalytics. They rate the dgree to which a company's enterprise business value is at risk driven by environmental, social and governance issues. The rating employs a two-dimensional framework that combines an assessment of a company's exposure to industry-specific material ESG issues. The final ESG Risk Ratings scores are a measure of unmanaged risk on an absolute scalre of 0-100, with a lower score signaling less unmanaged ESG Risk
